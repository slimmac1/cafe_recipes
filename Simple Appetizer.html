This is delicious and is always a hit to every party! Submitted by....Marissa T. Hamzy of Torrington, CT

1 round bread, comes in many variaties; all are very good
about 12-oz. Sharp Cheddar Cheese (shredded)
2 Cups of Hellmanns mayonnaise

Cut round bread out as if you were cleaning a pumpkin. Break cut out portion of bread into bite size pieces.

Mix shredded cheese with the mayonnaise. Pack cheese/mayo mixture into hollowed out round bread. Bake approximately 45-minutes at 350-degrees, until top is lightly browned.

Serve on platter with bite size pieces surrounding it.

For variation, add can of baby shrimp, or some crumbled bacon bits, or mix different types of cheeses together - i.e. Cheddar/Monterey Jack.

 
If your recipe calls for buttermilk, and you don't have it on hand, use 1 Tablespoons lemon juice OR white vinegar in one cup of sweet milk. Let stand for 5-minutes.
