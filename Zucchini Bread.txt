


Ingredients:
- 2 cups zucchini, grated
- 3 large eggs
- 1 cup olive oil
- 1-1/4 cup sugar
- 2 tsp vanilla
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 1/2 tsp baking powder
- 3 tsp cinnamon
- 1/8 tsp nutmeg
- 1 tsp salt
- 3 cups flour

Steps:
(1) Preheat oven to 350.

(2) In a large bowl, whisk eggs until thick and yellow. 
    Add olive oil, sugar, vanilla and zucchini until well-combined.

(3) In a separate bowl, combine dry ingredients. Slowly add to wet 
    mixture, stirring after each addition until ingredients well combined.

(4) Pour batter into well-greased bread pan.

(5) Bake for about 50 minutes. Serve warm with tea, or smeared with cream 
    cheese frosting.
